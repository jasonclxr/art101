ABOUT ME (Jason Blecman)

Heyo! I'm Jason! I'm a third year Art & Design: Games and Playable Media major.
My favorite hobbies include, programming, working on cars, tutoring, watching football
and making games. I've been working on games since I was about 11, and have always been
fascinated with the 3D world.

I've mostly expressed these passions through the game development platform Roblox,
where I've been able to make a pretty successful game called Football Universe. The game
has spawned a community with active player counts numbering around 900 to 1,200 active
concurrent players. I've made a lot of friendships around this community and learned a lot
about what it means to be apart of a group and a team player.
